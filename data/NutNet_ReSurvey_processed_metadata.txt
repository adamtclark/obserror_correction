SITE: NutNet or DRAGNet site name
SITE_CODE: NutNet or DRAGNet site code
NOTES: Notes associated with measurement (should be NA for all rows)
continent: continent of NutNet or DRAGNet site
country: country of NutNet or DRAGNet site
region: region of NutNet or DRAGNet site
managed: binary - is grassland at site managed?
burned: binary - is grassland at site burned?
grazed: binary - is grassland at site grazed?
anthropogenic: binary - is grassland at site human made or maintained (or natural grassland)?
habitat: habitat class for site
elevation: elevation for site (m)
latitude: latitude for site
longitude: longitude for site
experiment_type: NutNet or DRAGNet experiment?
block: block number
plot: plot number
trt: treatment (Control, N/P/K Fertilizer, Fenced, Disturbed, or interactoin)
first_nutrient_year: first year in which nutrients were added
species: species ID
family: species family
group: species functional group (graminoid/non-graminoid)
cover_1: cover for first survey
surveyor_1: surveyor ID for first survey
date_1: survey date for first survey
cover_2: cover for second survey (if applicable)
surveyor_2: surveyor ID for second survey (if applicable)
date_2: survey date for second survey (if applicable)
cover_3: cover for third survey (if applicable)
surveyor_3: surveyor ID for third survey (if applicable)
date_3: survey date for third survey (if applicable)
cover_4: cover for fourth survey (if applicable)
surveyor_4: surveyor ID for fourth survey (if applicable)
date_4: survey date for fourth survey (if applicable)
